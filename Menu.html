Chocolate is a type of sweet food,a brown food preparation of cocoa seed.It is made in the form of a liquid , paste or in a block,used as a flavouring ingredient in other foods.There are different types of chocolate,coming from different countries.The most famous European chocolate is the Swiss and Belgian.The unsweetened chocolate contains primary cocoa solids and cocoa butter in different proportions. Much of the chocolate consumed today is sweet chocolate,containing sugar.

Doughnut is a type of fried dough confectionary or dessert food.The doughnut is popular in many countries as a sweet snack ,that can be homemade or can be purchased in bakeries, supermarkets ,food stalls.It has a ring shape and has a hole in the middle. Various toppings and flavours are used for different types such as sugar, chocolate or mapple glazing.Dougnuts may also include water,leavening, eggs,milk,sugaring and other common natural artificial flavours. After frying,dougnuts are often topped.

Coffee is a brewed drink,prepared of roasted coffee beans. The plant has been exported to Africa to countries around the world. Coffee plants are cultivated today in over 70 different countries, primarily in the equatorical regions of America,South-East Asia and Africa. Coffee is slightly acidic and has a stimulating effect on humans because of its caffeine content.It can be presented in variety of ways- Espresso,caffe latte,cappuccino.It is usually served hot,although iced coffee is a popular alternative
